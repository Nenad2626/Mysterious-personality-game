Greetings everybody and welcome into the game of mystery :) .Rules for this game are simple.Game contains 15 levels.In the middle of blue square you have a headline with text-"Famous celebrity".Every level has a frame with picture hidden.Picture is covered with fields.You open the fields by clicking it.Clicks are limited.Once you start the level time will ticking.If you know the name of your mysterious personality,just type their name in input rectangle below the frame.For example-"Bill Gates".Name and last name must start with capital letter, otherwise answer will be incorrect.If answer is correct,picture will be revealed and text with new level will pop-out.Every fifth level adds more fields and more time in the game.Input is unabled for typing after time is over.


Good luck all of you :) .
